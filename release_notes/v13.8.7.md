# Changelog

Changes in v13.8.6..v13.8.7:

## Player — Expressive (V6)

### Portrait
- Bigger album art and controls anchored to the bottom above the queue bar
- Single action row: Share, Like, Download, Shuffle, Repeat, More (⋯ last)
- Wider Previous/Next buttons and a properly round Play/Pause button
- Removed duplicate Library/Lyrics from the player (kept on the bottom queue bar)
- Tighter spacing and center-aligned title, artist, and controls

### Landscape
- Correct wide layout: art on the left, controls on the right
- Larger album art; slightly smaller transport buttons
- Queue, Sleep timer, and Lyrics bar always visible along the bottom edge
- Shuffle and Repeat removed from landscape player controls

### Queue & lyrics
- Compact landscape queue header when the queue is expanded
- Lyrics screen stays on top in portrait and landscape (no longer hidden behind the player or queue)

## UI / Settings
- Expressive (V6) is the only player design; other themes removed from settings
- Existing installs migrate to V6 automatically
- Mini player sits above the bottom navigation bar

## Fixes
- Player controls no longer clipped or hidden behind the queue bar
- Less empty gap between portrait action row and bottom queue bar
