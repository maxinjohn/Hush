# Changelog

Changes in v13.8.6..HEAD:

## Player (Expressive V6)

**Portrait**
- Bigger album art
- Controls anchored to the bottom of the screen
- Single action row: Share, Like, Download, Shuffle, Repeat, More
- Wider previous/next buttons and a round play button
- Removed duplicate Library and Lyrics buttons from the main player (kept on the queue bar)
- Mini player sits above the bottom nav instead of overlapping it

**Landscape**
- Art on the left, controls on the right
- Queue, Sleep, and Lyrics bar built into the player
- Shuffle and repeat removed from landscape controls
- Compact queue header when the queue is expanded
- Lyrics open full-screen with correct layering over the player

## Settings

- Player theme locked to Expressive (V6) only
