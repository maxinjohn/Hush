# Changelog

Changes since v13.11.2:

## Highlights

- **Dedicated Car Expressive Player Layout**
  - Full-width cinematic artwork and PulseMatrix canvas presentation.
  - Title/artist overlay, compact elapsed/progress/duration row.
  - Large transport controls and single-row car actions.
  - Queue, Share, Like, Download, Shuffle, Repeat, Sleep, Lyrics, and More controls.

## Settings

- Added **Car Expressive Player Layout** toggle under Appearance.
- Added optional auto-hide artwork title setting.
- Added configurable title hide delay: 3, 5, 8, or 10 seconds.

## Car Player

- Title/artist can marquee once when too long, then fade after the selected delay.
- Touching the car player restores the title/artist and restarts the timer.
- Car-only visual arrangement leaves normal/mobile Expressive layout unchanged.

## Stability

- Search and low-memory improvements.
- Waze Bridge updates and reliability fixes.
